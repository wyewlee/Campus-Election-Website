2020

Digital Member of Parliament

Digital Parliament (Parlimen Digital)

Organized by youth-led non-government organizations, namely Challenger Malaysia, Undi 18, Liga Rakyat Demokratik and United Nations Association Malaysia (UNAM) youth. Parlimen Digital is the world’s first Digital Parliament initiated by youth, with 222 youth representatives representing the federal seats in Malaysia convene for a two-day debate session to discuss and pass new “laws” and “policies” on economic and education issues affecting youth in the country via the online platform. The virtual parliamentary seating which was powered by Microsoft Teams emulated an actual sitting including voting and passing of bills. The session was streamed live on 4 and 5 July 2020, recording more than 200,000 viewers for both days. Parlimen Digital was widely supported by politicians, ranging from ex-prime minister, ex-minister, ministers and members of parliament.
